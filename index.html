IMDS DIT Analyzer Dashboard

How to Pull IMDS Data:

  1. Open IMDS Screen 100 (Maintenance History).
  2. Pull a 7 or 10-day history for your target equipment.
  3. Press Ctrl+A to select all text on the screen, then Ctrl+C to copy.
  4. Click the text box below and press Ctrl+V to paste the data.
